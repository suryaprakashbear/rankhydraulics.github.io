Our Services

RANK HYDRAULICS is a full range of Professional Industrial services for your business


Rank Hydraulics, a diversified Engineering company providing a complete range of engineering products and has been serving the engineering industry for two decades, with utmost dedication, commitment, and transparency. Rank hydraulics is a registered manufacturer of High-Pressure Hydraulic Hose Assemblies. We are the Certified Hose Assembler for Parker & Gates Hoses. We have End to End OEM hose connections. We have a state of the art infrastructure with totally imported crimpers to crimp up to 4” dia. hoses in India and up to 16” dia. Hoses in Dubai. We are located in the city, within close proximity to ensure your non-stop production. We have branches in Kakinada (Andhra Pradesh), Hyderabad (Telangana), Dubai (UAE), and Frankfurt (Germany). We endeavor to provide services to Oil & Gas, Marine, Ship Building, Petrochemical, Construction, and Engineering industry in India, Middle East Asia, Europe, and Africa. Rank Engineers is a well –resourced company with unquenchable desire, striving for excellence, perfection, and total customer satisfaction. We directly service the end-users by offering exceptional supplies and services. We have been working closely with well-known principals, manufacturers, and suppliers committed to quality and service in all fields related to our core business. We are therefore committed to the Total Quality Management concept. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified company. We are transforming into excellence through continuously improving various aspects of our operations into an Integrated Business System.
